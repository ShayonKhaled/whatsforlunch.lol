what's what's for lunch?

a discord bot that posts the daily cafeteria menu to your server every weekday at 9:00 AM JST.

Disclaimer: This is an independent student project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the university or its cafeteria in any way.

why this exists

checking the cafeteria menu means digging through a weekly email, clicking a sharepoint link, and opening a pdf - every single day. i eat lunch at cafeteria pretty often. this bot skips all of that and just posts the menu where i already am.

in fewer words, it exists because i am lazy.

add it to your server

the bot is free to use. click the button below to invite it, once it’s in your server, run /subscribe in any channel to get started.
